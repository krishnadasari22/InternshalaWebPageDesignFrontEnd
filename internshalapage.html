Q. How will India’s Biggest Recruitment Fair work?

India’s Biggest Recruitment Fair features internship opportunities with a job offer. You need to register above, after which you can apply to the internships from 11th November. Once selected, you must complete your internship for a specified duration of 4-6 months and based on your performance, the company will offer a full-time job.

Q. Where will the Recruitment Fair take place?

The Recruitment Fair will take place online on Internshala from 7th-14th November.

Q. Do I need to pay any fee to register for the campaign?

No, it’s absolutely free :)

Q. Who is eligible to apply for these internships?

All the final year students and those who have recently graduated from any degree or stream are eligible to apply for these internships.

Q. What kind of internships will be available in the fair?

All the full-time and in-office internships in all major categories like - engineering, management, design, media, etc will be available in this fair.

Q. What will be the selection procedure of the internships?

Based on your application and resume submitted, you would be shortlisted by the employer. After shortlisting, you would then be contacted by the employer for further interview round.

Q. I am looking for a job instead of an internship. Are there any job opportunities in this fair?

The fair features internship opportunities (and NOT job opportunities). You are required to complete the internship to receive a job offer. 

Q. Can I apply for more than one internship?

Yes, you can apply for as many internships as you like, provided you meet the eligibility criteria.

Q. I have a question that has not been answered here, what should I do?

Please write to us at contest@internshala.com and we would be happy to help.
